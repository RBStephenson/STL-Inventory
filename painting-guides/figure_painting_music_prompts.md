## 📝 Tips for ElevenLabs Music

- Target **60–90 seconds** for YouTube background loops
- Add *"minimal arrangement"* if results feel too busy
- Add *"layered, cinematic orchestration"* if results feel too sparse
- Regenerate 2–3 times per prompt — results vary significantly between runs
